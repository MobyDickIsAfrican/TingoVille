How it works

How transactions Work

  • Step 1:You place order on the platform and a reference number for the order is generated
  • Step 2:Your order is processed by the shops the product belongs to
  • Step 3:Your order is then delivered to our fulfilment centre facilities and is readied for shipping
  • Step 4:Your order is now ready to be shipped. You will receive an email and sms informing you of the time the order will arrive.
  • Step 5:Your order is delivered to your doorstep by our delivery partners.
  • Step 6:The delivery partner receives your payment. Checkout our Returns Policy for information regarding product returns.

How to sell with us

Create a shop on our platform easily in under two minutes:

  • Step 1:Choose name of your shop and give a bio of who you are and what you sell
  • Step 2:You can choose to be a professional seller or a casual sellerYour order is processed by the shops the product belongs to
  • Step 3:A professional seller can sell an unlimited number of products, the product listings can remain on the platform indefinitely subject to receipt of a monthly subscription fee of R 200
  • Step 4:A Casual seller is able to list a limited number of products, and the products are removed from the platform after fulfilment of purchases. You can create a casual shop for FREE. Your order is now ready to be shipped. You will receive an email and sms informing you of the time the order will arrive.
  • Step 5:For rates and fees see our Rates and Fees page.
  • Step 6:All shop owners are verified and vetted to ensure the safety of transactions and consumers is upheld.
